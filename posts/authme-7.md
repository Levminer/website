Authme 1.0.0 was released more than 6 years ago. This release focuses on updating dependencies and improving encryption storage.

### Authme 7 TLDR:

- Updated encryption storage
- Updated dependencies

### Updated encryption storage

- Updated to latest `keyring` version, should be backwards compatible with previous versions.

### Updated dependencies

- Updated `esbuild` to a newer version.

You can download Authme from [GitHub](https://github.com/Levminer/authme/releases/tag/7.0.0) or from the [website](https://authme.levminer.com/).
